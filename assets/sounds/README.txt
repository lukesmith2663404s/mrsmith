SOUNDBOARD AUDIO FILES
======================

Place your sound files in this folder.

For new sounds, begin the filename with its keyboard key, followed by a space,
hyphen or underscore. Examples:

  Q question time.mp3
  B-buzzer.mp3
  P_pop.mp3

The page displays the remainder of the filename beneath the key.

After adding, removing or renaming sounds, double-click:

  update-soundboard.bat

Only one sound can be assigned to each letter.
